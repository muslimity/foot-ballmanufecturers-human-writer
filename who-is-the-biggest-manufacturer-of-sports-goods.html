Under Armour

Under Armour is the undisputed leader when it comes to sports-related goods. The brand was created in 1996 when Kevin Plank, a former University of Maryland football player and entrepreneur, decided to produce moisture-wicking shirts for his fellow athletes. Since then, Under Armour has earned the title of the world’s leading sports brand by investing in new technologies and manufacturing techniques to provide its customers with the best products possible.
