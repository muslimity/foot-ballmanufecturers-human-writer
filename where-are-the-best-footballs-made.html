Bauer

The first thing you may have noticed about a traditional football is that the ball is made from a single piece of leather and rubber. While this is partly due to the history of the game, it’s also because of the consistency of the materials. A single piece of high-quality rubber is much more consistent than a ball made out of several different pieces of varying quality.
